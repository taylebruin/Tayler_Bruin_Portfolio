Introduction

As a sports journalist for the renowned BYU Daily Universe, I had the privilege of covering the exciting world of BYU men's soccer during my first semester. Through captivating game summaries and engaging previews, I provided readers with insightful and comprehensive coverage of the team's journey, culminating in their conference and national championships. In my second semester, I expanded my skillset by transitioning to the video side of things. Each week, I created dynamic sports packages, showcasing my ability to produce compelling visual content. Additionally, I produced engaging social media stories, further demonstrating my versatility as a journalist. My time at the BYU Daily Universe has been an incredible learning experience and a valuable opportunity to hone my journalistic skills. I will finish my time at BYU this spring traveling to Palmyra New York to do a project on the 200 year anniversary of the Book of Mormon as my capstone course. I am proud to have contributed to the paper's exceptional reputation for excellence and look forward to continuing to produce high-quality content in the future.
