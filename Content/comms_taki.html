Microwaved Taki

This video was created for my Communications 312 class. The purpose of the project was to learn to use shot composition correctly. This meant using a wide shot first. Then going into a medium shot, and then three or more tight shots. After that sequence we were to start over. There was a small visual effect I added which was the smoke on the Taki. Finally, if you are wondering yes, we did actually microwave it and he actually ate the microwaved Taki.
2/10 would not recommend eating.
